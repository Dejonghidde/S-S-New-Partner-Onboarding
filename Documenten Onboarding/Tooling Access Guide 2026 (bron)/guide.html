Five rules that make
this fast and safe

Why we ask for access: audits, tracking, campaigns and experiments all start inside your tools. The sooner access is complete, the sooner the real work starts. These five rules apply to every tool in this guide.

1

Never share passwords by email

Every platform in this guide has an invite or partner flow, so you stay in control and can revoke access at any time. If a shared login truly cannot be avoided, we exchange it through a password manager, never in plain text.

2

One address for everything

Grant access to accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com. That address reaches your whole growth team at once, so access never depends on one person's account.

3

Grant the highest workable role

Admin rights let us set up tracking, audiences and campaigns without waiting on extra permission rounds. You can always scale a role down later.

4

Only what applies to you

Skip the tools you do not use. Your growth team confirms the exact list for your stack before or during the kickstart.

5

Stuck? We do it together

Send a message in our shared Slack channel and we will walk through it with you on a call, screen share included. No access question is too small.

Section 1

01Analytics & Measurement

Everything we do starts with reliable data. These four tools tell us what visitors actually do, so decisions run on evidence instead of gut feeling.

1.1Google Analytics 4

We need: Administrator (Editor is the workable minimum) · takes ± 2 minutes

  1. Go to analytics.google.com and click Admin (the gear, bottom left).
  2. Open Account access management. Account level gives us all properties in one go; property level works too.
  3. Click the + button and choose Add users.
  4. Enter accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com, select the Administrator role and click Add.
No GA4 property yet? No problem, we set it up for you. Grant us Tag Manager and website access and we take it from there.
1.2Google Tag Manager

We need: Admin on the account, Publish on the container · takes ± 2 minutes

  1. Go to tagmanager.google.com and open your container.
  2. Open the Admin tab and click User Management in the Account column.
  3. Click +, then Add users, and enter accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com.
  4. Set account permission to Admin and container permission to Publish, then save.
No Tag Manager yet? We create the container and deliver the code snippet for your site. One line to your developer and it is live.
1.3Google Search Console

We need: Full permission · takes ± 2 minutes

  1. Go to search.google.com/search-console and select your property.
  2. Open Settings in the left menu, then Users and permissions.
  3. Click Add user, enter accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com and choose Full.
No verified property yet? We verify it together via Tag Manager or a DNS record, five minutes on a call.

1.4Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity

We need: Admin · behavior recordings and heatmaps · takes ± 2 minutes

  1. Hotjar: go to Settings, open Organization > Members, click Invite members and add accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com as Admin.
  2. Microsoft Clarity: open your project, go to Settings > Team, click Add team member and add accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com as Admin.
Neither one installed? Clarity is free. We happily set it up through Tag Manager once we have access there.
Section 2

02Advertising

Your ad accounts are where budget meets results. For Google and Microsoft you send us an ID and we send the request, so you never touch a permission screen. For the social platforms you add us as a partner from your side.

2.1Google Ads

We need: to link your account to our manager account (MCC) · you send one ID, we do the rest

  1. Find your customer ID: click the profile icon in the top right of Google Ads. It looks like 123-456-7890.
  2. Send the ID to your S&S contact through Slack or e-mail. Just the ID, never login details.
  3. We send a link request from our manager account. You get a notification in Google Ads and by e-mail.
  4. Accept it under Admin > Access and security > Managers. Done.
Creating a fresh account? Set the currency and time zone with care: Google locks both permanently after creation. Skip the campaign wizard, we build the campaigns.

2.2Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

We need: partner access to your Business Portfolio assets · our Business Portfolio ID: 187500493833634

  1. Go to business.facebook.com and open Settings of your Business Portfolio.
  2. Under Users, click Partners, then Add and choose Give a partner access to your assets.
  3. Enter our Business Portfolio ID: 187500493833634.
  4. Select the assets that apply and grant full control per asset: your Facebook Page, ad account(s), pixel / datasets, catalog(s) and Instagram account.
  5. Click Save changes. We confirm from our side as soon as it lands.
No Business Portfolio yet? Create one at business.facebook.com with your business e-mail (not a personal profile), add your Page and ad account, then run the steps above. This keeps your ads independent from any individual's Facebook account, which protects you and us.
2.3LinkedIn (Page + Campaign Manager)

We need: page admin on your company page and Account Manager on the ad account

  1. LinkedIn ties admin roles to personal profiles. Your S&S contact sends you a connection request first.
  2. Page: open your company page as super admin, go to Settings > Manage admins and add your S&S contact as Super admin or Content admin.
  3. Campaign Manager: open your ad account, click the account name, then Manage access.
  4. Click Add user to account and add your S&S contact as Account Manager.
Billing is on you. Add a payment method under Billing center yourself. Without billing details LinkedIn will not deliver a single impression.

2.4TikTok Ads

We need: Admin in your Business Center plus Ad account admin · takes ± 3 minutes

  1. Go to business.tiktok.com and open your Business Center.
  2. Under Users > Members, click Invite member and add accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com as Admin.
  3. Assign your ad account to the new member with Ad account admin permission and confirm.
Prefer a partner link? That works too: under Partners, add us with our Business Center ID. Ask your S&S contact for the ID.
2.5Microsoft Advertising (Bing)

We need: to manage your account from our agency account · you send one number, we do the rest

  1. In Microsoft Advertising, open Settings > Accounts & Billing and copy your account number (looks like X1234567).
  2. Send it to your S&S contact through Slack or e-mail.
  3. We send a management request. Accept it via the link in the e-mail you receive, or under Settings > Requests.
No account yet? Often we can import your Google Ads setup into Microsoft in one afternoon. Ask your growth team whether the channel is worth it for your audience first.
2.6Other ad platforms (Pinterest, Snapchat, Reddit)

We need: admin-level access through the platform's own business or ads manager

  1. Pinterest: in your business account, go to Settings > Account access, invite accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com with Admin access to the ad account.
  2. Snapchat: in Business Manager, go to Members, invite accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com as Business Admin and assign the ad account.
  3. Reddit: in Ads Manager, open your business settings, invite accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com with admin permissions.
Exploring a new channel together? Perfect. Set up the account with a business e-mail (never a personal profile), then invite us before you build anything. We take it from there.

Section 3

03Website & CMS

Landing pages, pixels, experiments and conversion fixes all happen on your website. With the right role we ship improvements ourselves instead of adding tickets to your developer's backlog.

3.1WordPress

We need: our own Administrator user · takes ± 2 minutes

  1. Log in to your WordPress admin and go to Users > Add New User.
  2. Enter accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com as the e-mail, pick any username, and set the role to Administrator.
  3. Tick Send the new user an e-mail about their account. We set our own password through that link, so no password ever travels by mail.
3.2Shopify

We need: collaborator access · does not use one of your staff seats

  1. Find your collaborator request code: in Shopify admin go to Settings > Users > Security and copy the 4-digit code.
  2. Send the code to your S&S contact. Sharing the code gives us nothing yet, it only lets us send you a request.
  3. We submit a collaborator request from our Shopify Partner account.
  4. Approve it under Settings > Users (filter on Requests) and confirm the permissions.
3.3Other platforms (Webflow, Wix, Lightspeed, custom)

We need: admin-level access through the platform's own user management

  1. Invite accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com as admin through the platform's team or user settings.
  2. Custom-built website? Connect us with your developer in the shared Slack channel and we agree on a workable setup together.

Section 4

04E-mail, CRM & Automation

Your e-mail list is the channel you own. Whichever platform you use, the pattern is the same: invite our address through user management with an admin role.

4.1HubSpot

We need: full marketing access (Super admin works fastest)

  1. Go to Settings > Users & Teams and click Create user.
  2. Add accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com.
  3. Grant full access to the Marketing tools, or Super admin if you prefer one clean role.
4.2Klaviyo

We need: Admin or Manager

  1. Click your account name, then Settings > Users.
  2. Click Add new user and enter accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com.
  3. Select the Admin or Manager role and send the invite.
4.3Mailchimp

We need: Admin

  1. Click your profile icon and go to Account & billing > Settings > Users.
  2. Click Invite a user and enter accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com.
  3. Choose the Admin role and send the invite.
4.4ActiveCampaign

We need: admin rights

  1. Go to Settings > Users (bottom left).
  2. Click Add a user and enter accounts@sprintsandsneakers.com.
  3. Add the user to the Admin group and save.
Different e-mail or CRM platform? Same recipe: user management, our address, admin role. Mention it to your growth team so we tick it off on our side too.

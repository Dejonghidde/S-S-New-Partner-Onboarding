Sprints & Sneakers Proposal · not yet applied

B2C onboarding — copy review

Proposed text changes to make the questionnaire read less like it was AI-written and more like us. Nothing here is live yet — this is for your approval and feedback before we apply it.

What's changing & why

  • No more dashes. The questionnaire had 40+ em-dashes — the clearest "AI wrote this" tell. Replaced with commas and full stops.
  • Dropped the "not X, but Y" trick and the rule-of-three lists ("ad, email and hook").
  • Toned down the quotable one-liners on the between-section screens so they inform instead of perform.
  • Cut filler adverbs ("truly", "properly", "directly").
